Anj Tran is based in San Diego, CA and specializes in designing graphics for the digital sphere.

ABOUT

Anj has been doing art since she was very very young, inspired by her older sister. Originally, her work focused on traditional media such as pencil and ink drawings. Over time, Anj transitioned to digital art, utilizing software like Adobe Photoshop and Blender to create dynamic pieces. Her style is characterized by monchromatic elements featuring aspects of cybercore and a keen eye for detail.

With 7 years of experience in digital art, Anj's work includes social media banners, Twitch channel art, and more. She is passionate about creating visually appealing designs that effectively communicate her clients' messages.

Along her journey, she has also found interest in creating a variety of other art forms. These include architecture involving electronics, sewing plushies, carving, sculpting, and painting.

To commission Anj or any enquiries regarding usage, please contact directly.
